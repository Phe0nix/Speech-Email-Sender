📖 Complete Instructions

Getting Started

  1. Click the Start Listening button
  2. Your browser will ask for microphone permission - click Allow
  3. Start speaking your email subject
  4. Use the commands below to format your text

Text Formatting Commands

  • comma → adds a comma (,)
  • full stop → adds a period (.)
  • exclamation sign → adds an exclamation (!)
  • question mark → adds a question mark (?)
  • delete now → removes the last spoken item

Email Structure

  • line break → separates subject from body (important!)
  • paragraph break → adds a blank line in your text

Edit Existing Draft

  • subject mode → switch dictation back to the subject
  • body mode → continue dictation in the body
  • replace subject → clear only the subject and dictate it again
  • replace body → clear only the body and dictate it again
  • delete now → removes the last word from the active section

Sending Your Email

  • send email → opens your default email client
  • send gmail → opens Gmail in a new tab
  • Review the email preview and make any final edits before sending

Pro Tips

  • ✅ Always use "line break" after your subject for proper formatting
  • ✅ Speak clearly and naturally - pauses help with punctuation
  • ✅ Use the preview button to review before sending
  • ✅ Use undo if you make a mistake
  • ✅ The quick reference panel shows all commands at a glance
